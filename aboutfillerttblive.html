about

At TechTogether Boston, we're looking to shape the way women and non-binary people get involved with tech.
We aim to create an inclusive environemnt to encourage more underrepresented people to either get introduced to the world of technology or harness their skills to create projects of their own.
With the support of the technology community, industry, and individuals, we can chip away at the gender gap and empower anayone from someone with a non-technical major with an interest in tech, to seasoned hackathon veterans looking to build out a complicated and groundbreaking product with a little extra support.
